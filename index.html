FILE 01 / ABOUT

Where strategy
meets production.

I work at the intersection of three disciplines that are usually kept apart — digital design, brand graphics, and physical packaging — because the strongest brand experiences need all three to agree with each other.

For over five years I've helped FMCG brands, startups, and agencies turn ideas into shelf-ready packaging, scroll-ready websites, and campaign-ready visuals. My process is rooted in production reality: a label isn't finished until the die line checks out, and a landing page isn't finished until it converts on a phone in direct sunlight.

I move fluidly between Figma and Illustrator, between user flows and fold lines, because the discipline is the same — solve a real constraint, beautifully.
